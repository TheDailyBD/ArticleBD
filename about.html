About Us

Welcome to Chotokothai – your trusted platform for staying updated with the most relevant and trending news from Bangladesh and around the world. Our goal is to simplify your daily news experience by delivering concise, impactful updates that matter to you.

Every day, we carefully select 10 essential news stories from Bangladesh and 5 key international headlines, covering everything from politics and current events to lifestyle, technology, and entertainment. Each story is summarized in just 100 words and paired with a relevant image, ensuring you stay informed quickly and easily.

Whether you’re catching up after a busy day or want the most important updates at a glance, Chotokothai is here to bring you the news that truly matters. We’re more than just a news platform; we’re your companion in staying connected to your community and the world.
